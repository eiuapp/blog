5 A Few More Complex Functions

append-to-buffer

光标之前在 bufferB ，这里将是添加的位置。 光标跳到 bufferA. 在 bufferA 中, 选择区域，运行 M-x append-to-buffer ,然后，会在 bufferB 中添加。

copy-to-buffer

光标在 bufferA, 选择区域，运行 M-x copy-to-buffer bufferB-name RTN, 效果：在 bufferB 替换成了刚刚选中的区域内容

insert-buffer

光标在 bufferA, 运行 M-x insert-buffer bufferB-name RTN, 然后，会在 bufferA 处添加 bufferB 的内容

7

car and cdr

car = Contents of the Address part of the Register cdr = Contents of the Decrement part of the Register

cons

The cons function constructs lists; it is the inverse of car and cdr.

nthcdr and nth

The nthcdr function takes the CDR of a list repeatedly. The nth function takes the CAR of the result returned by nthcdr. It returns the Nth element of the list.

destructive

the non-destructive function: car cdr cons nthcdr nth. the destructive function(change the original list): setcar setcdr.
